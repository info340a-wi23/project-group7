About Us

We are a bunch of Huskies who hike! We created this webpage to combat issues we have with the current hiking apps in out area. Our main goals are to create an app that has:

  • A filterable map of local hikes
  • Hike-specific information and user-shared reports
  • Training tips for hikes based on difficulty

We hope it is helpful, and happy trails!
